Best Free PDF Tools for Students 2026

Students constantly work with PDFs: lectures, textbooks, coursework, theses. Let's explore which free tools will help you work efficiently with PDF files without spending money on paid software.

Why Do Students Need PDF Tools?

In modern education, PDF has become the standard format for sharing study materials. Professors distribute assignments in PDF, university portals accept submissions in PDF, and e-textbooks are published in PDF. Here are typical tasks students face:

  • Compressing papers for upload — university portals often restrict file sizes
  • Merging documents — assembling a thesis from multiple parts
  • Removing pages — deleting draft or unnecessary pages
  • Converting — from Word to PDF for final submission
  • Adding page numbers — numbering pages according to requirements
  • Splitting files — breaking textbooks into chapters for convenient reading

PDF Tools Overview

PDF Compression

The compression toolreduces PDF file size while maintaining visual quality. This is especially useful when a university portal won't accept files above a certain size.

Real-life example: a term paper with scanned appendices weighs 15MB, but the portal accepts a maximum of 5MB. Compression will reduce the size to an acceptable level.

Merge PDF

The merge tool lets you combine multiple PDF files into one document. Invaluable when assembling a thesis from text, appendices, and scans of signed documents.

Word to PDF Conversion

The Word to PDF converter transforms .docx documents into PDF format. Most universities require final versions of papers to be submitted in PDF.

Page Removal

The page removal tool lets you quickly remove unnecessary pages. Useful when you need to remove a draft cover page or extra appendices.

Page Numbering

The numbering tool adds page numbers in the desired position. Page numbering is a standard requirement for most term papers and theses.

Split PDF

The splitting tool breaks a large PDF into multiple files. Useful for splitting a textbook into chapters or extracting individual sections.

PDF to Images

PDF to JPG and PDF to PNG — convert PDF pages to images for inserting into presentations or other documents.
